Grumpy Cat Meme

Origin:

Grumpy Cat is the nickname given to the late Tardar Sauce, a cat belonging to former waitress Tabatha Bundesen. Due to her grumpy face he was made a meme.

Why she looks grumpy:

She was known for her permanently "grumpy" facial appearance, which was caused by an underbite and feline dwarfism.
